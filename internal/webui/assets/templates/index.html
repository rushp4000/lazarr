How Lazarr works

1Grab — instant, nothing downloaded When Sonarr/Radarr grabs a release, Lazarr records it and places a file link in your library. Nothing is added to your TorBox account.
2Play — streamed on demand When you press play (Plex, Jellyfin…), Lazarr adds the torrent to TorBox and streams the bytes straight to your player. First play takes a few extra seconds.
3Clean up — automatic After it sits unwatched for the idle window, Lazarr removes it from TorBox again. Your library file keeps working — the next play just repeats step 2.

Content availability

Lazarr checks every grabbed release against TorBox daily (read-only, never adds anything). Items marked Unavailable were purged from TorBox’s cache — playing them would fail. “Re-search” deletes the dead link so your arr notices the missing file and grabs a fresh copy.

TorBox account compliance

Lazarr promises to never hoard content on your TorBox account. The audit compares what TorBox says is on your account with what Lazarr believes it added; a leak means something was left behind and should be investigated.
